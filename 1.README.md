📺 Bright Car Sales — Subscription Growth & Viewership Analytics

Customer Value Management (CVM) Analytics Project focused on increasing subscriptions, improving customer engagement, and driving sustainable growth.

📖 Project Overview

BrightTV's CEO has set a strategic objective to grow the company's subscription base during the current financial year.

To support this objective, the Customer Value Management (CVM) team requires data-driven insights into customer behaviour, subscription patterns, demographics, and content consumption trends.

This project provides a comprehensive analysis of subscriber and viewership data to identify opportunities that can increase customer acquisition, retention, engagement, and overall subscription growth.

🎯 Project Aim

To provide detailed analytical insights that help BrightTV increase subscriptions and improve customer value through a deeper understanding of viewer behaviour, demographics, and content consumption patterns.

📌 Business Objectives

The project aims to answer key business questions such as:

Who are BrightTV's most valuable subscribers? Which demographic groups are most likely to subscribe? What viewing behaviours drive subscription conversions? Which provinces contribute the highest subscription revenue? How can customer engagement be improved?
